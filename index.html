The Palm Harbor Riders started by Mike and Kelly Wicker is a 55+ cycling group located on the west coast of central Florida. It is designed for older adults who enjoy casual cycling, each other's company, and the great Florida outdoors. This page will explore the wonderful world of cycling in Florida!
